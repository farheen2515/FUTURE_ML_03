Chocolate Cake

⏱ Prep Time: 45 mins | 🍴 Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix dry ingredients together.
  3. Add eggs and butter, whisk well.
  4. Pour into a cake tin.
  5. Bake for 30–35 mins.
  6. Cool, decorate & serve.
